Last updated: October 24, 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. Our Apps do not require account registration, email addresses, or any form of personal data input.

We also do not access or collect data such as:

  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or files

2. No Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

3. Data Storage

All data generated or entered within the App (for example, notes, accounting records, or model releases) is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to that data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are suitable for users of all ages, including children.

5. Data Deletion

Because we do not collect or store any user data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete. Users can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App from their device.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use of our Apps means you accept the updated policy.
